SafeLink App

October 2024

Project Overview

SAFE LINK is an emergency response mobile application integrated with a satellite communication device (SAFE LINK Beacon). It provides immediate access to vehicle services, medical help, police, and fire departments, especially in areas without mobile coverage. The app uses advanced features like automatic accident detection and satellite connectivity to ensure constant support during emergencies.

Key Features

🚨

Automatic Accident Detection

Instantly notifies emergency services

📡

Satellite Communication

Maintains connectivity in no-network areas

🚑

Emergency Service Access

Quick contact to medical, police, fire, and vehicle assistance

📱

User-Friendly Interface

Easy navigation for all age groups

Challenge & Solution

Challenge

SAFE LINK faces challenges like high device cost, reliance on external hardware, limited battery life, satellite coverage issues, and strong competition from services like Apple SOS and Life360.

Solution

SAFE LINK can offer affordable pricing plans or EMI options, design a compact and user-friendly beacon, optimize battery usage for longer life, collaborate with reliable satellite providers for better coverage, and highlight unique features like automatic accident detection to stand out from competitors.
